About Us

Medinova Hospitals is widely recognized as the pioneer of private healthcare in Victoria, and was the country's first corporate hospital. It started as a 150-bed hospital and today, operates 9200 beds across 64 hospitals. A forerunner in integrated healthcare, Medinova has a robust presence across the healthcare spectrum.
